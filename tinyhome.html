Where did this interest start?

My interest in tiny homes started with mindless scrolling through pinterest. What started as ideas for a room remodal quickly spiraled into discovering tiny homes. A spark of interest was held so I continued to research it! I learned about the different types, such as: skoolies, converted school buses; van builds, converted white vans; and trailer builds, houses build directly on top of trailers. This passion is one of my future dreams!

What are my plans for it?

My plan is to convert a school bus, so I will be living in a skoolie! My plan is to convert an eight-window bus and include a bed, kitchen, shower/toilet combination, and a dinette! I have been doing extensive research for this and have a basic understanding of how to do the conversion by myself! I hope to do my conversion before I go to college so I can have a home while I study!
