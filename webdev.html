Where did my interest come from?

The first time I created a website was in 5th grade. I got to do it for a school project using wix.com. It was very basic, and I wasn't actually coding, but I did enjoy the experience. Later, when given the chance in 8th grade, I signed up for a Web Development class. I don't exactly remember the name, but I do know that I learned HTML and CSS to make my own website. I distinctly remember my website being about golden-doodles, which is a breed of dog. I had a fantastic time with it, and that's what encouraged me to sign up for an Intro to Comp Sci class!

What are my aspirations for web development?

I hope to go into UX design and development. I find web development to be a lot of fun and I have always had an interest in human behaviour and psychology. I have a logical and analytical brain as well as being a creative and expressive person. Finding a pairing for these attributes was always hard. It was either I go into the computer science field and become a data analysist and software developer, or I can go into the art field and become a freelance artist or graphic designer. Interactive media and UX design are the combinations of that!
