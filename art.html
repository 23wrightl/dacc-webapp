Where did my interest start?

My mom first signed me up for an art camp at The Art Barn. There I discovered a passion to create art! It started off with me taking more art classes in middle school in favor of continuing orchestra. I took Ceramics I and II, Painting, and Drawing. I took all that they offered and even dropped a Culinary class for it! Once I got to highschool I took Art I, Drawing I and II, and Digital Design I. I also dove into my school's theater program. I participated in my middle schools theater for all three years I attended, then became a stage manager my freshman year at my highschool. I am still currently a stage manager in my theater program! I also took Stagecraft as a class!

What are my favorite mediums of art?

My absolute favorite medium is graphite; although, I have dabbled in painting, water color, and colored pencils! I have created many pieces in graphite with a specialty in portraits! My interest in portraits started when my Art I class had a final assignment of drawing an eye. Every since I have expanded from that and created entire portraits! I also have drawing objects such as a purple lego piece drawn in colored pencil and a graphite architecture piece of my multiple dream snapshots.
