Where did my interest come from?

I have always embraced my creative side. At first, I thought that I would become an engineer or entrepreneur. I would design and prototype my many ideas. Then, I discovered the world of computer science and got to design my own video games and interactive cards. I loved the idea of creating something with just an idea in my head. Thus, my reason for loving design!

What are my favorite tools for design?

My favorite tools for design are the Adobe applications. I started learning Adobe InDesign in my Intro to Journalism class. Using this, I wrote a series of different types of journalistic writings and designed and created a magazine! After I took my Intro to Journalism class, I decided to take a Digital Design class. In that class I learned to use Adobe Illustrator. Using Illustrator, I created multiple projects, including a poly-portrait, a flat design winter scene, and a travel poster for Crater Lake! After that class, I became very interested in interactive media. I decided to take a summer college class at Columbus State Community College called Fundamentals of Interactive Media. There I learned to use Adobe Photoshop to create various posters, Adobe Animate to create basic animations, and Adobe Dreamweaver to create websites with HTML and CSS. Overall, the Adobe applications and systems are my favorite to use!
