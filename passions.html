Design


The definition of design:

Merriam-webster defines design as "to devise for a specific function or end." My definition of design is to plan out a creative idea, that serves a purpose to someone or something. This purpose must be used to help, never hurt. When I design things, I don't think about it much. Normally I just spill all my random thoughts onto a page.
